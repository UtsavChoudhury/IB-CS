1. These are rules for transaction managment to ensure that a database remains consistent

Atomicity: A transaction fully happens or doesn’t happen.

Consistency: Each transaction leaves the databse in a valid state.

Isolation: Transactions don’t interfere with one another.

Durability: Once committed, changes are permanent.
